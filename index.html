Script 1: Warm Lead - Buyer Inquiry

Use When: Prospect has inquired about a property online, filled out a buyer form, or requested information.

Goal: Qualify the buyer and book them for an intro call with the agent.

üéØ STAGE 1: Opening (First 10 Seconds)

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] calling from [Agent Name]'s team. How are you today?"
[PAUSE - Wait for response. Build micro-rapport]

"Great! I'm calling because you inquired about [property address / homes in the area] online. Is that something you're still interested in?"
‚ùì STAGE 2: Qualification Questions
YOU:
"Wonderful! Just to make sure we're showing you homes that fit what you're looking for, can I ask you a few quick questions?"

Q2 - Area:
"And what areas are you focusing on? I saw you looked at [property area] - is that your top choice, or are you open to other neighborhoods?"
Q3 - Property Type:
"What type of property are you looking for? How many bedrooms and bathrooms do you need?"
Q4 - Budget:
"And what's your budget range? Have you spoken with a lender yet to get pre-approved?"
Q5 - Motivation:
"Can I ask what's driving your move? Are you relocating, upgrading, first-time buyer?"

Use When: A property listing expired without selling.

YOU:
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] calling from [Agent Name]'s office. How are you today?"
YOU:
"I noticed your listing at [address] recently came off the market. I wanted to reach out to see if you're still interested in selling, or if your plans have changed?"

‚ùì STAGE 2: Empathy + Discovery

Script 8: No-Show Follow-up

Use When: Prospect missed their scheduled appointment.

Goal: Reschedule without sounding annoyed or desperate.

‚ö†Ô∏è Mindset Shift: Don't take no-shows personally. 60% of no-shows will reschedule if you follow up correctly. Stay professional and empathetic.
üéØ STAGE 1: Immediate Follow-up (Within 15 Minutes)
FIRST CALL (Within 15 min of missed appt):
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Agent Name]'s office. We had you scheduled for [time] today - just wanted to make sure everything's okay! Give me a call back when you get this."
[Keep it concerned, not accusatory. They might have had an emergency]
IMMEDIATE SMS:
"Hi [Name], we missed you on the call today at [time]. Everything okay? Let me know if you need to reschedule - [Agent Name] is still happy to help!"
üìû STAGE 2: Follow-up Call (If They Answer)
YOU:
"Hi [Name]! I tried calling earlier - we missed you for your appointment at [time]. Is everything alright?"
[Listen to their reason. Common responses: forgot, emergency, busy, changed mind]
IF Legitimate Emergency:
"I completely understand - life happens! [Agent Name] is still excited to speak with you. Would tomorrow work, or should we look at next week?"
IF They Forgot:
"No worries! I know everyone's busy. Since [Agent Name] already prepared [market analysis / property list / custom plan] for you, can we find a quick 15 minutes in the next day or two?"
IF Vague/Evasive:
"I understand. Can I ask - are you still interested in [buying/selling], or is now not the right time?"
[If they're still interested, reschedule IMMEDIATELY. Don't say "let me know when works"]

üéØ No-Show Recovery Tips:

  • Make rescheduling easy: Offer 2-3 specific times, don't ask "when works for you"
  • Set a boundary: After 2 no-shows, deprioritize the lead
  • Update your CRM: Note the no-show and follow-up attempts
